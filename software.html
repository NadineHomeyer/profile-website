FEW within the AMBER Suite of Biomolecular Simulation Programs

Functionality

Free energy workflow tool for binding free energy calculations for several ligands binding to the same receptor.

Available types of free energy calculations:

  • MM-PBSA (Molecular Mechanics Poisson-Boltzmann Surface Area)
  • MM-GBSA (Molecular Mechanics Generalized Born Surface Area
  • LIE (Linear Interaction Energy analysis)
  • TI (Thermodynamic Integration)

A tutorial demonstrating the functionality of FEW is available at the Amber Tutorials website.

References

  • Homeyer, N.; Gohlke, H. J. Comput. Chem. 201334, 965-973.
